Hi, I'm Dawid

I’ve been diving into graphic software for years, playing around with tools like Affinity, Sony Vegas, DaVinci Resolve, and, since around 2019, Blender.

When it comes to 3D modeling, I basically just go for anything that sparks an idea — whether it’s something random I imagine or something cool I come across and think, “Yep, I gotta make that.”

Detail is everything to me, so I’m always pushing myself to make each render better than the last, raising the bar with every project. I mostly render in Cycles, which, well, doesn’t exactly love my not-so-powerful computer—so yeah, a lot of my work ends up as single frames.
